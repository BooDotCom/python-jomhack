split(): converts a string into a list(array) of substrings based
on its delimiter (separator)

usually is whitespace (space bar)

strip():
- a string method removes whitespace by default from a string.
- can be specified to remove certain characters.

eg:
text = "Hello World"

parts = text.split()

print parts
result: ['Hello', 'World']

to read code, go from bottom to top
to fix code, go from top to bottom
